Summary

Serial entrepreneur and technology executive with 50+ years of experience spanning actuarial science, enterprise IT infrastructure, public education governance, and AI safety. Founded and led multiple companies serving Fortune 100 and public-sector clients. Co-founded the Beckman Laser Institute and Medical Clinic at UC Irvine, now a world-leading center for pediatric laser treatment. Currently building Hallucinations.cloud, an AI safety platform that simultaneously queries eight large language models for cross-validation, misinformation detection, and reliability scoring. Lifelong commitment to community service across healthcare, education, and family safety.
