About Brian

For over half a century, I've worked with numbers—numbers that mattered

For over fifty years, I built enterprise systems that Fortune 100 companies trusted with their most critical operations. As founder of RemoteNet Corporation, I designed unified benefits platforms serving organizations like Northrop Grumman. My actuarial background taught me one essential truth: when the stakes are high, you verify everything.

In 2023, I turned that same scrutiny toward artificial intelligence. After asking ChatGPT over 5,000 questions, I discovered a troubling pattern: these systems fabricate information with absolute confidence, presenting fiction as fact—with no way for users to know the difference.

That discovery led me to build Hallucinations.cloud and the H-LLM multi-model verification platform. By querying eight AI models simultaneously, we expose inconsistencies that no single model reveals on its own. I also host "Brian & AI: Hallucinations in the Wild"—a podcast helping general audiences understand when AI tells the truth and when it misleads.

After five decades of building systems that organizations trust, I'm now focused on making AI worthy of that same trust.

"In a world where machines talk like humans, we need to know when they're telling the truth—and when they're not."
